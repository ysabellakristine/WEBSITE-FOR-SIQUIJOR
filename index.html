Siquijor Province

The Land of Beauty and Magic


Source: siquijorprovince.com

Siquijor is the third smallest province in the Philippines.

It has beautiful beaches, activities, caves, and sites to see! Not only that but people go here not only for the sights but for its infamy in South East Asia as a centre of witchcraft, magic, and folk healing. Ask anyone in their 30's and above about this province and I guarantee you that they will either say it is a place full of witches or aswang.

I would know, since both of my parents on separate occasions said it was an island of witches.
